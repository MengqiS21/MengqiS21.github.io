Academic Homepage

Mengqi Shi

Master Student in HCI, University of Washington

I am currently pursuing a Master of Science in Technology Innovation (Connected Devices) at the University of Washington, Seattle. I received my Bachelor's degree in Communication with minors in Informatics and Data Science from the University of Washington, where I was a three-time Annual Dean's List recipient.

Currently, I am a research intern at the AI4SG Lab at the National University of Singapore (NUS), supervised by Dr. Yi-Chieh (EJ) Lee and Tianqi (Holly) Song. I also serve as a Research Assistant at the ACE Lab (University of Washington), advised by Dr. Jacob O. Wobbrock, Dr. James A. Fogarty, and Mingyuan Zhong. Previously, I have held research positions at the Department of Human Centered Design and Engineering (HCDE), and gained industry experience as a User Research Intern at Xiaomi Inc.

My research interests include human-computer interaction related to accessibility and responsible AI. I am passionate about combining empirical studies of human behavior with the design of inclusive, human-centered technologies to lower barriers to participation and address overlooked social and emotional needs. Additionally, I am interested in how digital platforms and social media influence collective meaning-making and public perception.

I enjoy engaging in interdisciplinary collaborations that bridge the gap between social computing and physical prototyping. Feel free to email me if you are interested in collaborating, discussing research ideas, or just making friends! :)
