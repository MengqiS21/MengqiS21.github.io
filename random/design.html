GuardianBear

Pediatric healthcare technology · wearable emergency response concept

Link

GuardianBear is a child-friendly emergency response system designed for children with ITP (Idiopathic Thrombocytopenic Purpura). The concept combines a bear-shaped hemostatic tool with a parent-facing app for bleeding-status monitoring, location tracking during emergencies, and communication with emergency or medical support.

  • HCI
  • Healthcare Design
  • Wearable Concept
  • App Concept

Mooncake Enclosure for MUJI Hotel

Packaging and interaction design · TECHIN 511

Link

A mooncake box concept designed to extend the calm, natural, and minimalist visual language of the MUJI Hotel brand. The enclosure uses wood material, laser-cut detailing, and sliding screen-inspired doors to create a slower, more deliberate opening interaction.

  • Branding
  • Laser Cutting
  • Enclosure Design
  • Prototyping

SurfaceHelper

Flat-surface cleaning helper · TECHIN 511 final project

Link

A small desktop cleaning robot concept for removing light stains from stovetops and countertops. The team explored sketches, component tests, low-fidelity iterations, modeling, mechanism design, and interchangeable robot fronts to make a utilitarian cleaning tool feel more playful and customizable.

  • Product Design
  • Mechanism Design
  • 3D Printing
  • Prototyping
